## Sleep
- Target bedtime: 9:40 PM (5:40 AM wake-up, 8 hours needed)
- Body wants to stay up late — chronic tension with early work schedule
- No eating in last 2 hours before bed (heart rate stays elevated 60–65 bpm otherwise)
- Heart rate at 55 bpm or below = body ready for sleep
- Big dinners 4–7 PM preferred; prevents late snacking
- Sleep aids (strategic, not daily): magnesium (post-exercise), trazodone (heaviness/duration), melatonin (onset), valerian root (occasional)
- Off-schedule nights: combine trazodone + melatonin ~30 min before target bedtime
- Work break naps on yoga mat in dark room = genuinely restorative; replacing with AI study is counterproductive
- Cascade failure: late night → undersleep → crash → missed exercise → everything degrades
